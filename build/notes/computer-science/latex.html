LaTeX

Intro

A high-quality typesetting system. Mostly used for technical and scientific documents. Basically the standard in academia.

Example

\documentclass{article}
\title{Cartesian closed categories and the price of eggs}
\author{Jane Doe}
\date{September 1994}
\begin{document}
   \maketitle
   Hello world!
\end{document}
